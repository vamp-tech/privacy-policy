VAMP is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and other related services.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

b. Sensitive Personal Information

Due to the nature of our services, we may collect:

  • Health or biometric data (blood drive dates and places)

c. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, software version)

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our app

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Your consent

We may share your information with:

VAMP does not touch the blood draw. Blood drive signups and preliminary paperwork are handled externally, without our involvement.

We do not sell your sensitive personal information.

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Encryption (in transit and at rest)

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage users to review and protect their account credentials.
